Polyhedral design with blended n-sided interpolants

closed quadmesh of arbitrary topology -> G2 or C-infty vertex-interpolating surface
- vs. Zorin -> no approximation, no subdivision
- vs. Fang  -> no normals, no Least, no edge-surfaces (ribbons)

- non-closed meshes, boundary / cross-derivative interpolation

1. Alapotlet regularis esetben (quadmesh, ha nem az akkor CC),
   masodfoku Bezier feluletekkel, blend variansok (G2, C-infty)
   -> interpolacio nelkul is mukodik, de kevesbe szep
2. Kiterjesztes QGB-vel, de haromszogre nem jo
3. Kiterjesztes Midpointtal
4. Normalvektorokkal jobb hatarok

Problema: ez igy nem G1, mert az n-oldalu feluleteknel a reszek parameterezese nem folytonos
